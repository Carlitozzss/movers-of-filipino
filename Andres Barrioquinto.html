Andres Barrioquinto"Impulse of Desire" (2022)

Art enthusiasts will instantly recognize an Andres Barrioquinto piece—they’re often the ones in vivid color, with stop-you-in-your-tracks images, and an overall quality that is strangely transfixing. Much has been said about him in art circles here and abroad, and all the praise is well-deserved. The artist has mounted multiple exhibitions in many of the world’s art capitals, including Japan, Singapore, and the United States, and he is a current favorite for many collectors here and abroad.
